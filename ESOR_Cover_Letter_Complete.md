**Project Overview**

DIKE AI is a sophisticated educational equity analysis tool that leverages artificial intelligence to evaluate assignments and learning materials across six critical dimensions of educational equity: socioeconomic barriers, time constraints, cultural inclusivity, accessibility needs, digital divide considerations, and learning support resources. The platform provides educators with actionable, data-driven recommendations to create more inclusive learning environments.

**Rationale and Impact**

The inspiration for DIKE AI stems from the growing recognition that educational inequity remains a persistent barrier to student success. Traditional methods of identifying equity issues in curriculum design are often subjective, time-consuming, and rely on educators' existing awareness of potential biases. Our solution democratizes access to equity analysis by providing instant, comprehensive evaluations that anyone can use.

The impact of DIKE AI extends beyond individual assignments. By systematically identifying potential barriers, the tool helps institutions:
- Reduce dropout rates among marginalized student populations
- Improve academic performance across diverse learner groups
- Foster inclusive campus cultures that celebrate diversity
- Meet compliance requirements for accessibility and equity standards
- Save educators valuable time while improving educational outcomes
